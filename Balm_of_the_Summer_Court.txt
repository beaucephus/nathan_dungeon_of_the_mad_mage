====== Balm of the Summer Court ======
Created Saturday 04 April 2020

You are a font of energy that offers respite from injuries.
You have a pool of fey energy represented by a number of d6s equal to your druid level.

As a bonus action, you can choose an ally you can see within 120 feet of you and spend a number of those dice equal to half your druid level or less.
Roll the spent dice and add them together.
The target regains a number of hit points equal to the total.
The target also gains 1 temporary hit point per die spent.
